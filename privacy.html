Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 28, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Clawdbot Music ("we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use our internal analytics tool.

1. What We Do

Clawdbot Music is an internal analytics tool that accesses YouTube Analytics data to generate performance reports for our own YouTube channel. This application is not intended for public use.

2. Information We Access

We access the following data through the YouTube API:

  • YouTube Analytics data (view counts, audience retention, traffic sources)
  • YouTube channel data (video metadata, public statistics)

This data is accessed in read-only mode. We do not modify, delete, or publish any content on your behalf.

3. How We Use Information

The accessed data is used solely for:

  • Generating internal performance reports
  • Analyzing audience retention patterns
  • Improving our content strategy

4. Data Storage

Analytics data is stored locally on our private servers and is not shared with any third parties. Authentication tokens are stored securely and are not exposed publicly.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share any accessed data with third parties.

6. Google API Services User Data Policy

Clawdbot Music's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

7. Revoking Access

You can revoke Clawdbot Music's access to your Google account at any time by visiting your Google Account Permissions page.
